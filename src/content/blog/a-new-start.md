Welcome to my corner of the internet.

This is the first post on my brand new website. After many iterations and attempts, I've finally landed on something I'm happy with — a clean, warm digital home that feels like mine.

Personal websites have always felt like a lost art to me. In a world dominated by social media platforms, there's something special about having your own space on the internet. A place where you control the design, the content, and the experience.

This site will grow with time. I plan to write about whatever crosses my mind — technology, music, books, projects I'm working on, and life in general. No set purpose, just a digital public journal.

Thanks for stopping by. More to come.
